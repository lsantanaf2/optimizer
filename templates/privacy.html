⚡ Optimizer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Optimizer ("we", "us") is a Meta Ads management tool available at optimizer.xn--trfego-qta.com. This policy describes what data we collect through the Meta Platform, how we store it, and how you can request its deletion.

1. Data we collect

When you connect your Facebook account, we collect and store:

  • Meta access token — used to act on your behalf on the Meta Marketing API;
  • Your Meta user ID and email — to identify your account;
  • Ad account identifiers you choose to import;
  • Campaign metadata and performance insights (campaign/ad names, spend, impressions, clicks, conversions) — fetched on demand to render dashboards and reports.

We do not collect data about your friends, followers, or any person other than you.

2. How we use the data

Platform Data is used exclusively to provide the service to you: creating ads, adjusting budgets, and displaying performance metrics of ad accounts you already manage. We do not sell, license, rent, or share Platform Data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the tool.

3. How we store and protect it

  • Access tokens are encrypted at rest (AES-128-CBC + HMAC via Fernet) before being stored;
  • Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database (Supabase) with row-level security;
  • All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS);
  • API usage respects Meta's rate limits, with server-side throttling and caching.

4. Data retention and deletion

Access tokens are kept only while your Meta connection is active and are deleted when they expire or when the connection is removed. Campaign insights are fetched on demand and cached briefly (minutes to hours) — we do not build long-term archives of Platform Data.

You can request deletion of all your data at any time:

  • Removing the Optimizer app in your Facebook Business Integrations settings automatically triggers our data deletion callback; or
  • Emailing us at ads.lsantanaf@gmail.com — we will delete your data and confirm within 30 days.

5. Your rights (GDPR / LGPD)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and may withdraw consent at any time, by contacting ads.lsantanaf@gmail.com.

6. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.

7. Contact

Data controller: Optimizer — ads.lsantanaf@gmail.com
